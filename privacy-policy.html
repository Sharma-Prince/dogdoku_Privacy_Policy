Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

Thank you for playing Dogdoku ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Game.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Dogdoku does not require you to create an account or provide personal information in order to play.

Automatically Collected Information

Depending on the services enabled in the Game, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash reports
  • Anonymous diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (where applicable)

2. Third-Party Services

The Game may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (if advertisements are enabled)
  • Google Play Games (if enabled)
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and may collect information in accordance with those policies.

3. How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used to:

  • Provide and improve the Game
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Analyze application performance
  • Display advertisements (where applicable)
  • Maintain and improve user experience

4. Children's Privacy

Dogdoku is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by trusted third-party service providers solely for operating and improving the Game.

6. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help protect information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

  • Reset your advertising ID from your device settings.
  • Manage application permissions through Android Settings.
  • Uninstall the Game at any time.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
